    Evaluation Rules
  1. 5% Daily Drawdown
      2. You must not have a 5% drawdown based on your balance or equity on your account within a 24 hour period starting 00:00 server time.
  2. 12% Overall Drawdown
      3. Your account must not go down by 12% (realized/unrealized) to balance or equity.
  3. 8% profit target phase 1
      4. You need to reach the profit target of 8% without breaching any of the other rules within a 30-calendar day period. Your time starts when you open your first trade on your FOREX trading account.

  6. No trading restrictions
